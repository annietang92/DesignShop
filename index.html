The Spring 2014 Education DesignShop:

Using Design Thinking to Re-Envision the Education System


MIT Education DesignShop Trailer

When: May 3-4, 9am-5pm
Where: TBD
Application Deadline: April 16

The Education DesignShop is a 2-day workshop for interdisciplinary teams to learn design thinking and apply it to fixing the education system. We will bring together 100 educators, engineers/designers, policy experts, and students to create innovative strategies for implementing solutions in the world of education.

Winning deliverables will be personally delivered into the hands of key education stakeholders.
