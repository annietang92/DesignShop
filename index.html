The Spring 2014 Education DesignShop:

Using Design Thinking to Re-Envision the Education System


MIT Education DesignShop Trailer

When: May 3-4, approx. 8am-6pm
Where: Cambridge Innovation Center, 5th floor
Applications are now closed, but you can folow us on twitter @EduDesignShop.

The Education DesignShop is a 2-day workshop for interdisciplinary teams to first learn design thinking and then apply it to the education system. We will bring together 25 of each students, educators, policymakers, and industry personnel (100 participants in total) to create innovative strategies for implementing solutions in the world of education.

Winning deliverables will be personally delivered into the hands of key education stakeholders at the event, and specially designed prizes will help teams bring their ideas to fruition.
